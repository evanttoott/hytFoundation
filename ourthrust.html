Our Thrust

Education

Quality education is for all. As we pursue for a better tomorrow, we must nurture our dreamers upon a strong educational foothold. And, with that, HYT Foundation has come up with several programs that would bring quality education even to the most unfortunate.

Enhancement

Stock knowledge is still knowledge. And, learning never stops even when we grow old. Everyday, we learn new things that could eventually help us in our future endeavors. It is one of HYT Foundation’s goals to make learning an everyday commitment, especially among dreamers.

Experience

As it goes, learning never comes without experience. In exploring new things, we experience how something looks, tastes, or feels. And, that makes our lives more interesting and valuable. The Dream Academy, HYT Foundation’s flagship program, is one that serves to prove how experience can shape dreamers to become their better selves.

Entrepreneurship

Learning does not end with books. It even goes far to engaging in business and innovation. One’s experience in life determines one’s skills in entrepreneurship. For this, HYT Foundation has set up programs that would further improve entrepreneurial skills among dreamers.

Endurance

Life is boring without sports. In addition to mental nourishment, our physical bodies need to work and to sweat in order to keep us fit and healthy. A number of programs have been made by HYT Foundation with the purpose of fostering sportsmanship and collaboration among dreamers.

Exploration

Dreamers are said to be at their highest point in life of exploring the unexplored. Adventure is indeed their cup of tea. From across different facets of life, many things have been discovered, and some have even left us in awe. It is the objective of HYT Foundation to build avenues for dreamers to discover more and to gain wisdom.

Empowerment

Life is boring without sports. In addition to mental nourishment, our physical bodies need to work and to sweat in order to keep us fit and healthy. A number of programs have been made by HYT Foundation with the purpose of fostering sportsmanship and collaboration among dreamers.

Enlightenment

As dreamers strive to fulfill their talents and potential, it is essential that we have their backs. Ultimately, our youngsters today will become tomorrow’s leaders. And, with that, HYT Foundation has put up programs that will ensure our dreamers would not be left on their own, as they work on their successes.
